Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ApplyNinja.

How does the AI apply for me?

Our autonomous agents use Selenium to navigate job portals like LinkedIn. They log in, search for jobs matching your criteria, and fill out applications using data extracted from your resume.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your credentials are encrypted and processed locally on your machine during the automation session. We do not store your LinkedIn password on our servers.

Can I stop the bot at any time?

Absolutely. The dashboard features an emergency "Stop Bot" button that instantly halts all automation processes.
